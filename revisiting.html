Revisiting
Flash Photographic Festival
The Handsome Daughter, Winnipeg, Manitoba
April 1–30, 2022

Curated by Diana Thorneycroft.

Revisiting was presented as one of two featured Emerging Exhibitions in the 2022 Flash Photographic Festival. Curated by Diana Thorneycroft, the exhibition explored memory, place, and the shifting relationship between landscape and personal history through photography. The exhibition was presented at The Handsome Daughter in Winnipeg, Manitoba, with custom reclaimed wood frames built by Lake Manitoba Woodworks.
