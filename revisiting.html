Revisiting

Flash Photographic Festival
The Handsome Daughter
Winnipeg, Manitoba
April 1–30, 2022

One of two featured Emerging Exhibitions at the 2022 Flash Photographic Festival.
Curated by Diana Thorneycroft.

Revisiting is about how we understand our past, as seen through how we capture and represent places from our present. Through the act of returning to and photographing spaces from my childhood and recent histories, an investigation of the connection between site, memory and presence occurs.
