The Booth Photos

We're Still Here: 50 Years of the Winnipeg Film Group
Platform Centre for Photographic + Digital Arts
Winnipeg, Manitoba
2024

Commissioned by the Winnipeg Film Group.

The Booth Photos is a commissioned photographic series documenting the projection booth of the Dave Barber Cinematheque in celebration of the Winnipeg Film Group's 50th anniversary. The photographs were exhibited as part of We're Still Here: 50 Years of the Winnipeg Film Group, an archival exhibition presented at Platform Centre for Photographic + Digital Arts.
